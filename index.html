Our Story

Our Legacy

Three Generations of Excellence

A legacy rooted in 1954, when Ajit Singh laid the foundation of Delhi Manufacturing Pooja Mandirs in Delhi. Guided by his principles of craft, discipline, and social contribution, the family business grew across generations into what is now Mandir Emporium — a distinguished name in luxury pooja mandir.

1954
Ajit Singh founds Delhi Manufacturing Pooja Mandirs in the heart of Delhi.
1990s
Second generation expands into Pooja Mandir, establishing a reputation for quality craftsmanship.
1998s
Gurdwara Rakab Ganj Sahib 24K Gold Palki Sahib project done by Jasbir Singh.
2004s
Gurdwara Nanak Piao Sahib 24K Gold Palki Sahib crafted by Jasbir Singh.
2011s
Gurdwara Bangla Ganj Sahib 24K Gold Palki Sahib redesigned and the interior done by Deco Furns.
2014s
Gurdwara Sis Ganj Sahib 24K Gold Palki Sahib and Outer Top Gumbad 24k Gold and the Pora Sahib underneath designed and crafted.
2021s
Third generation introduces bespoke design services and institutional project management.
2021s
Furniture Block,Kirti Nagar showroom opens — Delhi's premier mandir destinations.
2022s
Plot no.28, Furniture Block,Kirti Nagar showroom opens — one of Delhi's premier mandir destinations spanning 2700+ sqft².
2026s
1/56 Timber Block,Kirti Nagar showroom opens — one of biggest mandir destinations spanning 5400+ sqft².
Today
Mandir Emporium serves 37,000+ satisfied clients with 10,000+ design options across all categories.

How It Works

01
Consultation
Meet our design team to discuss your vision, space requirements, and preferred materials and finishes.
